Patates Yanhi

Patates Yahni (Greek potato stew) is a traditional Greek recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into 2.5 cm chunks
  • 1 medium red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 3 ripe tomatoes, peeled and grated
  • 1/2 a cup olive oil
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp freshly ground pepper
  • 2â€“3 tbsps chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Spread potatoes evenly in a large casserole dish.
  3. Mix it all together
  4. Roast in the preheated oven, uncovered, until just tender, about 30 minutes
